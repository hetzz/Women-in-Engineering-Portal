What is WE?

Despite the fact that women famously played a pioneering role in the early decades of programming and technology, the participation of women engineers in the technology sector globally still only stands at 26%, as per the Bureau of Labor Statistics. The primary objective of this program is to address the gender imbalance in technology by grooming promising talent among women engineering students to advance and contribute to the field. Prioritising inclusion, the program is designed to support talented women engineers from under-privileged and disadvantaged backgrounds, Designed by TalentSprint, supported by Google, this one-year experiential program aims to nurture 600 women college students over 3 years, of which 100 will be in the first year. The program is a combination of summer coding bootcamps, live online classes, ongoing mentorship, certification and team-based projects to enhance problem solving and computational thinking. It also provides exemplary participants a chance to get career opportunities at Google and its partners.
